Baking

Techniques

There are many techniques used in baking especially for certain recipes to turn out correctly. You are also going to need to have the correct measurements for the recipe. Techniques help with getting things like eggs and butter fluffy for cakes and other recipes. The way you mix things depending on what you are making is very important to the recipe and make sure it comes out the way it supposed to. Knowing different techniques will help greatly in improving baked goods and knowledge on the subject. It would be important for someone to know if they were going to or wanting to have a bakery. Some baking techniques used are:

  • Creaming
  • Mixing
  • Floding
  • Whisking
  • Rubbing in
  • Kneading
